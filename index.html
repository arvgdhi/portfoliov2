About Me

Background

I recently passed my 12th grade at Velammal Vidyalaya Annexure (CBSE) and I have been interested in aerospace and AI for a few years now. My main goal is to work on systems that connect space science with artificial intelligence.

I have spent a lot of time actually building things rather than just reading about them. I configured a conceptual satellite, built an optical telescope mostly from 3D printed parts, and researched topics like interplanetary habitation and Helium-3 energy. I have also explored PCB design, 3D modeling, and AI agent development.

I like taking complicated problems apart and figuring them out from scratch, then building something real with what I learn.

Projects

Personal AI Assistant

I built a local AI agent on a Raspberry Pi that can run tasks and hold conversations. It reads and summarizes emails, organizes them automatically, and has a Discord interface built with OpenClaw.

3D Printer Remote Control

I set up OctoPrint so I can remotely monitor and control my 3D printer. I can check the status of a print in real time without being physically near the machine.

Custom Built Telescope

I designed and assembled a high resolution telescope for sky observation. Most of the parts are 3D printed and it runs on a Raspberry Pi. I use it independently and it can zoom up to 160x.

Satellite Design Project

I designed a conceptual satellite system covering structure, subsystem planning, power, and telemetry. It is built around a Raspberry Pi with multiple sensors and cameras attached.

Conversational AI Model

I built a conversational AI to replicate a fictional character's speech style, inspired by the novel Project Hail Mary. It uses a Llama 1B model and I plan to run it locally on a Raspberry Pi.

Aquaponics Ecosystem

I combined biology and engineering to build a self-sustaining aquaponics system where fish and plants support each other in a closed loop.

Research

Interplanetary Habitats

I did independent research on what it would take to create sustainable living systems for humans in space. My findings were submitted to the National Space Society (NSS) and the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CIASC).

Satellite and Payload Development

I took part in the design and assembly of ultra-light payloads and femto satellites as part of a large student aerospace project. I got hands-on experience with payload systems, experimental design, and launch processes.

Helium-3 Energy Applications

I looked into the challenges of extracting Helium-3 from the Moon and explored how it could be used as a clean energy source in the future through fusion-based systems.
